Your Data, Your Control

Here's exactly what we do - and don't do - with your information

What We Do

  • Create anonymous, aggregated statistics (e.g., "10 alumni in Maastricht")
  • Track Master's destinations for programme development
  • Connect you with other alumni if you both agree
  • Always ask before featuring you publicly

What We Never Do

  • Share your personal information with third parties
  • Display your name publicly without explicit consent
  • Sell or monetize your data in any way
  • Make it difficult to opt out

Questions about how we handle your data? Just ask.
